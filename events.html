FAQ's

Am I eligible to donate? Check Eligibility Here
Can I earn points for getting my friends and family to donate? Yes, One point per donation.
What if I am not in Pittsburgh?
  • Donating blood or plasma at any organization globally can earn Greek Week points for your chapter. Just send proof (like an 'I donated' sticker) to bbl9@pitt.edu
  • We will award points for outside donations until Greek Week in the spring.
  • Find an outside drive: Vitalant, Red Cross
How do I make sure my donation counted for Greek Week points?
  • On campus donation: mention your organization during check-in
  • Off campus donation: Send proof (like an 'I donated' sticker) to bbl9@pitt.edu
